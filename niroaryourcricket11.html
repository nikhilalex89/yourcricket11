The Story Behind YourCricket11.fun

Being a lifelong cricket fan, YourCricket11.fun is my 3-year-long dream project. Like every cricket lover, I’ve always wanted to build my perfect playing 11. But all I found online were fantasy league platforms with limited player choices and commercial intentions.

I wanted more freedom — to pick based on form, memory, or pure admiration. So I built it myself, for fans like me.

What is YourCricket11.fun?

  • Create your own Indian Playing 11 using current players.
  • Rebuild your favorite historic Indian XIs.
  • Mix legends and current stars into a dream all-time 11.
  • Select for specific formats: Tests, ODIs, T20s.
